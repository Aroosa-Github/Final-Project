Scatter Matrix for Diabetic vs Non-Diabetic

For probablistic analysis of the data, a simple approach might be just to use the top correlated variables and investigate them further. In this case we have used Glucose, BMI and Age. We are not using pregnancies because we don't want to pay attention to gender.

Diabetic Patients
